Oven Fresh Bakery started as a morning biscuit with a hot cup of milk tea. Now, Over Fresh Bakery has been serving other baked good categories such as fruit cake, banana bread, doughnuts, bagels, cheese danish, cakes and more kinds of biscuits for almost 32 years. We serve the sweet and strong coffee, chai and latte along with baked goods. Our tea leaves are hand picked in Ilam from Nepal and granulated for tea bags and sealed. Taste the mixture of South Asian tea with your favorite american bagel with a simple click on your items and add it to your cart to get it delivered or pick up in our bakery located in Fort Worth, TX.
